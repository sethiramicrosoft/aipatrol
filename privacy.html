Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 May 2025  ·  Last updated: 25 May 2025  ·  URL: https://sethiramicrosoft.github.io/AIPatrol/privacy.html

AIPatrol is designed to operate entirely on your device. The extension does not transmit the content you type or paste, and does not send analytics, telemetry, or usage data to us or any third party. Your browser vendor independently processes installation and update data under their own privacy policy.

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

AIPatrol is a browser extension developed and operated by Sudhakar Ethirajulu, an independent software developer.

Contact: sethirajulu@hotmail.com
Source code: github.com/sethiramicrosoft/aipatrol

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom and wish to make a formal data-subject request, please email the address above with the subject line "Privacy Request".

2. Data We Do Not Transmit

AIPatrol does not transmit to any external server:

  • The text you type or paste into AI tools
  • Any detected sensitive data (API keys, passwords, personal information)
  • Your browsing history or the websites you visit
  • Your IP address or location
  • Your name, email address, or any identity information
  • Usage statistics, analytics, or telemetry of any kind
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

3. What the Extension Processes Locally

To provide warnings, AIPatrol accesses the text you type or paste into prompt fields on supported AI sites at the moment you attempt to submit it, solely to evaluate it locally against detection rules. This scanning is a form of data processing under applicable law, but it happens entirely in your browser's memory on your device.

  • Scanned text is not written to persistent storage
  • Scanned text is not transmitted to us or any third party
  • AIPatrol does not contact any AIPatrol-operated server
  • All detection rules ship inside the published extension package — no remote code is loaded

The extension also stores your local preferences (e.g., enabled/disabled state) in your browser's extension storage. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data.

Legal basis (GDPR): Processing is carried out on the basis of your consent given by installing and enabling the extension (Art. 6(1)(a)) and our legitimate interest in providing the protective functionality you requested (Art. 6(1)(f)).

AIPatrol requests the following permissions:

5. Third-Party Services

AIPatrol itself does not send your data to any third party. However, please note:

  • Browser/extension store: The Chrome Web Store and Microsoft Edge Add-ons store may process device and download information to distribute and update the extension, under their own privacy policies.
  • AI platforms: The AI sites you use (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) process your prompts under their own privacy policies. AIPatrol warns you before you send; it does not change what those platforms do with data you choose to send.

6. Children's Privacy

AIPatrol is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction — 13 to 16 under GDPR Art. 8). If you believe a child has used the extension in a way that raises a concern, please contact sethirajulu@hotmail.com.

7. Data Retention

AIPatrol does not operate servers that hold your data, so there is no server-side retention. Your local extension preferences are retained in your browser's storage until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data. Scanned text is processed in memory only and is not retained after the check completes.

🛡️ AIPatrol

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 May 2025  ·  Last updated: 25 May 2025

✅ AIPatrol does not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your data — ever. All processing happens entirely inside your browser, on your device.

1. Who We Are

AIPatrol is a browser extension that warns users before sensitive data (such as API keys, passwords, and personal information) is submitted to AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and others. It is developed and maintained as an independent software product.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

AIPatrol does not collect any of the following:

  • The text you type or paste into AI tools
  • Any detected sensitive data (API keys, passwords, personal information, etc.)
  • Your browsing history or the websites you visit
  • Your IP address or location
  • Your name, email address, or any identity information
  • Usage statistics, analytics, or telemetry of any kind
  • Cookies or any persistent tracking identifiers

3. How Detection Works (Fully Local)

When you paste text or submit a prompt to an AI tool, AIPatrol runs pattern matching entirely within your browser using JavaScript. The analysis:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to any external server, API, or cloud service
  • Is discarded immediately after the check completes
  • Does not persist anywhere — not in browser storage, not in memory after the check

The extension operates entirely offline. No internet connection is required for detection to function.

4. Permissions Explained

AIPatrol requests the following browser permissions. Here is exactly why each is needed:

  • Read and change data on specific websites — Required to inject the content script that monitors text input on supported AI sites (e.g., chatgpt.com, claude.ai). The extension only runs on the listed AI platforms — not on all websites.
  • Display notifications — Used to show the warning banner when sensitive data is detected. No notification content is stored or transmitted.
  • Storage — Used only to store your local settings (such as whether the extension is enabled). No sensitive data is ever written to storage.

AIPatrol does NOT request permissions to read all websites, access your tabs, read your browsing history, or communicate with external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

AIPatrol does not integrate with, send data to, or communicate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud providers. There are no third parties involved in the operation of this extension.

6. Children's Privacy

AIPatrol does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As no data is collected at all, the extension is safe for users of all ages.

7. Data Retention

Because we collect no data, there is nothing to retain, delete, or export. Your local extension settings (e.g., enabled/disabled state) are stored in your browser's local storage and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since AIPatrol collects no personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, delete, or port. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, California, or any other jurisdiction with data protection laws, AIPatrol is fully compliant by design — there is simply no data to govern.

9. Open Source Transparency

AIPatrol's detection rules and logic are available for inspection. The patterns used to detect sensitive data are bundled with the extension in a human-readable rules.json file. You can inspect exactly what the extension scans for by viewing the extension's source files in your browser.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. Any future version that begins collecting data will require explicit user consent and will be clearly communicated before taking effect.

11. Known Limitations

Microsoft Edge restricts third-party browser extensions from running on Microsoft-owned websites, including copilot.microsoft.com. This is a browser-enforced policy outside our control. AIPatrol cannot monitor activity on Copilot when using Microsoft Edge. For full Copilot coverage, users may install AIPatrol on a Chromium-based browser such as Google Chrome or Perplexity Comet.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at sethiramicrosoft@github.com.
